Basketball

Basketball is one of the most widely viewed sports in the world. It is a team sport that involves two teams of five active players each trying to score points against one another by throwing a ball through a 10-ft high hoop (the basket) under organized rules. Basketball is a fast-paced game that requires players to constantly be mindful of their surroundings in order to ensure that their team maintains possession of the ball and has optimum opportunities to score and win.

Baseball

Baseball is a game that is played with three basic pieces of equipment: a bat, ball, and glove. The goal of the game is to have the most team members succeed in running around a four-point diamond without being taken "out" by the opposing team. When a team is able to get three of the opposing team members "out," the two teams switch positions on the field and the opposite team begins to try to get the most team members around the diamond without being taken out.

Soccer

Association football, commonly known as soccer, is a sport played between two teams of eleven players with a spherical ball. It is played by 250 million players in over 200 countries, making it a very popular sport. The object of the game is to score by using any part of the body (except the arms and hands) to direct the ball into the team's goal. The opposing team's main objective is to prevent this from happening, while at the same time trying to direct the ball into their own goal.

Lacrosse

Lacrosse is a combination of basketball, soccer and hockey. It is a truly exciting sport. Lacrosse is fast-paced game that is constantly full of action. Long sprints up and down the field with abrupt starts and stops, and precision passes and dodges are routine forms of action in both men's and women's lacrosse. Lacrosse is played with a stick, called a crosse, which must be mastered by the player to throw, catch, and scoop the ball. It is becoming an increasingly-popular sport in the U.S.
